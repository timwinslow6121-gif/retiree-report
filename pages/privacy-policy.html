Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who We Are

The Retiree Report is an educational website operated by a licensed Medicare insurance agent serving North Carolina and South Carolina. This site provides plain-language Medicare information and connects visitors with a licensed agent — no middlemen, no call centers.

Information We Collect

We collect information only when you voluntarily provide it — for example, when you subscribe to our newsletter or reach out by phone or email.

  • Email address — collected when you subscribe to the newsletter
  • Name — if you provide it when subscribing or contacting us
  • Phone number — only if you call or text us directly

We do not use contact forms that collect personal information without your explicit action. We do not collect sensitive health or financial data through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used only for the purposes you'd expect:

  • Sending the newsletter you signed up for
  • Responding to questions or consultation requests you initiate
  • Providing Medicare guidance you've asked for

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone.

Newsletter

If you subscribe to The Retiree Report newsletter, your email address is stored through our email service provider. You can unsubscribe at any time using the link included in every email. We will honor unsubscribe requests promptly.

Cookies and Analytics

This site may use basic analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the site — things like which pages are most visited and what devices people use. This data is aggregated and anonymous; it is not tied to your identity. No tracking cookies are used for advertising purposes.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Third-Party Links

Some pages may link to external websites (such as Medicare.gov). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any site you visit.

Children's Privacy

This site is intended for adults approaching or in retirement. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under the age of 18.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure. We encourage you to use caution when sharing personal information online.

Your Rights

You may request to view, correct, or delete any personal information we hold about you by contacting us directly. We will respond to reasonable requests promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out directly — contact information is on the Work With Me page.
